Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites take between 2-6 weeks to complete, depending on complexity. Simple landing pages can be ready in 1-2 weeks, while e-commerce sites with custom functionality typically take 4-6 weeks.

Do you provide website hosting and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive hosting and maintenance packages. This includes secure hosting, regular backups, security updates, and technical support to keep your website running smoothly.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Absolutely! We specialize in website redesigns that improve both aesthetics and functionality while preserving your existing content and SEO value.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, all our websites are built with responsive design principles, ensuring they look and function perfectly on all devices - from smartphones to desktops.

Do you provide SEO services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive SEO services including on-page optimization, technical SEO, content strategy, and local SEO to help your website rank higher in search results.

What's included in your website packages?

Our packages include custom design, responsive development, content implementation, basic SEO setup, training, and 30 days of post-launch support. Additional services like e-commerce, advanced SEO, and ongoing maintenance are available as add-ons.

Can I update the website myself after it's built?

Yes, we build websites with user-friendly content management systems (like WordPress) that allow you to easily update content, add blog posts, and manage your site without technical knowledge.

Do you offer e-commerce solutions?

Yes, we develop custom e-commerce websites with secure payment processing, inventory management, and customer account functionality using platforms like WooCommerce and Shopify.

What happens after my website launches?

We provide 30 days of complimentary support to address any issues. After that, we offer ongoing maintenance plans to keep your site secure, updated, and performing optimally.
