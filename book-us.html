Booking Questions

What is your booking process?

Our booking process is simple: contact us with your requirements, we'll discuss your vision and schedule, then we'll send you a booking confirmation with all the details.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for portrait sessions and 3-6 months in advance for weddings to ensure availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 48 hours notice for cancellations. For weddings and large events, please refer to our specific cancellation policy in the contract.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, we require a 50% deposit to secure your booking date. The remaining balance is due on the day of the shoot.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and digital payment methods like PayPal and Venmo.
