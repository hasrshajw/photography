Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, bookings, pricing, and more.

Your Questions Answered

General Questions

1 What are your working hours?

For booking and inquiries, we are available from 9 AM to 9 PM. For event work and shoots, our team operates 24/7 based on your requirements.

2 Do customers need to visit your studio?

Not necessary. All bookings can be done online through WhatsApp, call, or website. Clients may visit the studio only if they prefer discussing in person.

3 Do you provide both indoor and outdoor shoots?

Yes, we offer indoor, outdoor, home shoots, and on-location event coverage.

4 How early should someone book?

We recommend booking 7–10 days in advance to secure your preferred slot.

Packages & Pricing

5 Do you offer different packages?

Yes. Packages are created based on customer requirements, event type, and budget.

6 Do you have budget-friendly options?

Yes, we provide affordable packages without compromising quality.

7 Are prices negotiable?

Yes, prices can be slightly adjusted depending on the event and workload.

8 Do you charge extra for reels, drone shots, or albums?

Yes, these add-ons come with separate charges, depending on the level of creativity and output required.

Bookings

9 How can customers book your services?

Clients can book through: ✔ Call ✔ WhatsApp ✔ Website inquiry

10 Do you accept last-minute bookings?

Yes, but only in special urgent cases, depending on team availability.

11 What is the advance payment required?

A 50% advance is mandatory to confirm the booking.

12 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI and Cash.

Event Day Questions

13 How many photographers/videographers attend the event?

It depends on the event size — from a single shooter to a full team.

14 Do you carry backup equipment?

Yes. We always carry backup cameras, lenses, and storage to avoid any technical issues.

15 What if the client delays the event timing?

We follow the timing rules mentioned in our Booking Policy. Extra hours may be chargeable depending on the delay.

16 Do you charge extra if the event extends?

Yes. Extra hourly charges apply when the event goes beyond the booked duration.

Delivery & Output

17 How long does delivery take?

Final edited photos and videos are delivered within 30 days.

18 Do you provide teasers or early previews?

Yes, we offer sneak-peek photos or reels depending on the package.

19 How do you deliver photos and videos?

We deliver through: Google Drive, Private download link, Pen drive (optional), Printed Album (if included in package)

20 Are albums included in every package?

No. Albums are included only in the packages that mention them.

21 Do you charge extra for album pages?

Yes. Extra pages beyond the package limit are chargeable per page.

RAW Photos

22 Do you provide raw photos?

Raw photos are given only if the selected package includes them.

23 Do you provide raw video footage?

Yes, but only if the package includes it. Otherwise, raw video is not provided.

Editing

24 Can clients choose photos for editing or album design?

Yes. Clients can select their favourite photos for editing and album work.

25 How many revisions do you allow?

We offer one free revision. Additional revisions are chargeable.

26 Do you charge extra for extra edits?

Yes. Advanced edits, re-edits, or creative requests may carry additional charges.

Cancellation & Refunds

27 What is your cancellation policy?

Refund is given only if no work has started. Once work is in progress, advance is non-refundable.

28 Is there a fee for changing the event date?

Rescheduling within 15 days is allowed. Closer changes may incur a small fee depending on availability.

29 Is the advance ever refundable?

Only if we haven't started any work related to your event.

Travel & Location

30 Do you travel to other cities?

Yes. We cover events across Andhra Pradesh and nearby states.

31 Do you charge travel fees?

Sometimes. Travel charges apply based on distance and transport requirements.

Team & Experience

32 How experienced is your team?

Our team has over 20+ years of experience since 2000.

33 How many events have you covered?

We have covered hundreds of events including weddings, birthdays, outdoor shoots, and corporate programs.

34 Do you have female photographers available?

Yes, female photographers can be arranged on request.

Client Rights & Privacy

35 Can clients use the photos anywhere after full payment?

Yes. After full payment, clients receive full rights to use their photos publicly.

36 Do you use client photos for marketing?

Only with clear permission from the client.

Special Shoots

37 Do you do baby photoshoots?

Yes.

38 Do you do pre-wedding shoots?

Yes.

39 Do you do outdoor couple shoots?

Yes.

40 Do you do corporate and professional shoots?

Yes.

Others

41 Do you provide same-day editing?

Yes, but only in special packages, at extra cost.

42 Do you offer express delivery?

Yes, express delivery is available with additional charges.

43 What makes your studio better than others?

20+ years of experience, Small, skilled team, Affordable pricing, Heartfelt, emotional photography, Fast delivery, Professional behaviour, Backup equipment & reliable workflow

44 Do you offer seasonal discounts?

Yes. Discounts are available during off-seasons and special festival periods.

45 Do you take responsibility if someone copies your photos?

Once photos are delivered and made public, we are not responsible for third-party misuse. Clients are advised to watermark photos if required.
